Improvement and Feature Ideas: Challenges

3. Perform ingredient validation in view, before submitting the form. (Warn the user before submitting)

Advance Improvements:

6. Weekly meal planning feature: Assign recipes to the next 7 days and show on a weekly calender;

7. Get nutrition data on each ingredient from spoonacular API (https://spoonacular.com/food-api) and calculate total calories of recipe.
